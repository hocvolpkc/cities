About the assignment.

This is a website template from templated that I have altered for my Academy of Entertainment and Media (AME) Course. This website is made to make certain cities appealing to someone who is looking to travel.

London, England

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom,with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants. London is set on the Thames River. It holds one of the most iconic towers; the Big Ben.

Seoul, Korea

Seoul is a metropolitan arean full of large modern sky scrapers. This city is fairly modernized. Within the city there is a mixture of architecture. While there are many large sky scrapers there are also many old styled buildings like temples, palaces, and street markets.

Coron, Philippines

Coron is a first class tropic city that is widely known as a tourist attraction. Here there are so many things to do from diving and exploring the japanese shipwrecks that have sunken on Palawan waters years ago to relaxing in a spa with abosolutley nothing to worry about.
